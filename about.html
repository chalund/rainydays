Our History

Rainyday was established in 2004 and now one of Norway's leading outdoor store.We offer our customers a unique combination of premium brand expertise, selection, availability and the best prices.

We also emphasize durability and being suitable for a range of different weather types ensuring our customers are comfortable whatever adventure you are going on.

Our goal is to find the best solution for you when it comes to clothing so you can keep pushing the comfort zone in any kind of weather.
